CARE-22

Product Overview

Care-22 is a professional-grade health support system for the avian industry. It utilizes a unique dual-formulation approach—combining bio-active drops and therapeutic granules—to provide a comprehensive shield against physiological stress and immune challenges.

Specialized Packaging

Master Clinical Box: Contains 10 Unit Boxes
Unit Content: 1x Therapeutic Drops + 1x Concentrated Granules

Target Species

Broilers Layers Breeders Fancy Birds

Mode of Action

• Phase 1 (Drops): Rapid absorption through the mucosal lining to provide immediate metabolic stabilization during acute stress periods.

• Phase 2 (Granules): Sustained-release action targeted at gut health and long-term immune system modulation.

Key Benefits

  • Superior immune system defense
  • Optimizes bird growth & stamina
  • Synergistic effect of two formulations
  • Proven effective in large-scale flocks
