Carminato Colic

Product Overview

Carminato Colic is a specialized oral liquid designed for immediate relief from gas accumulation, stomach pain, and bloating in livestock. Its fast-acting formula neutralizes rumen gas and stabilizes digestive comfort.

Target Species

Cattle, Buffalo, Sheep, Goats, and Horses.

Available Packing

60 ml Bottle 120 ml Bottle

Key Benefits

  • Rapid gas neutralization
  • Instant relief from colic pain
  • Restores smooth gut motility
  • Prevents bloating complications

Indications

  • • Gaseous Colic and Flatulence
  • • Acute and Chronic Bloating
  • • Abdominal spasms and pain
  • • Indigestion-related stomach distress

Dosage & Administration

Cattle & Buffalo 100 ml – 120 ml
Sheep & Goat 30 ml – 60 ml

* Administer orally. Repeat dose after 3–4 hours if symptoms persist.

Storage & Safety

  • • Store in a cool, dry place away from heat
  • • For veterinary oral use only
  • • Shake well before each use
