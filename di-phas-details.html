FAGAS DI-PHAS+

Product Overview

Fagas Di-Phas+ is a concentrated 18% Di-Calcium Phosphate mineral supplement designed for large-scale dairy and beef operations. It ensures rapid correction of calcium and phosphorus deficiencies, improves structural integrity, and sustains high milk and meat output under commercial conditions.

Composition

Di-Calcium Phosphate (18%)
High bioavailability Calcium & Phosphorus in balanced physiological ratio.

Key Benefits

  • Rapid correction of calcium & phosphorus deficiency
  • Supports strong skeletal development in high-yielding animals
  • Enhances milk production and milk fat percentage
  • Improves growth rate and body mass in beef cattle
  • Prevents metabolic disorders caused by mineral imbalance
  • Ideal for intensive and commercial feeding programs

Indications

  • • Milk fever and hypocalcemia prevention
  • • Weak bones, lameness, or joint disorders
  • • Poor feed conversion in high-producing herds
  • • Delayed growth and skeletal deformities
  • • Mineral deficiency during peak lactation

Mode of Action

Di-Phas+ supplies bioavailable calcium and phosphorus that integrate into bone mineralization, regulate neuromuscular activity, and stabilize enzymatic and hormonal systems essential for sustained production, fertility, and metabolic health.

Dosage & Administration

Dairy Cattle 80 – 120 g / Day
Beef Cattle 50 – 90 g / Day
Calves & Young Stock 25 – 40 g / Day

* Mix thoroughly with concentrate or mineral feed

Target Species

Dairy Cattle, Beef Cattle, Buffalo, Calves, Commercial Herds

Storage & Safety

  • • Store in a dry, cool environment
  • • Keep tightly sealed to avoid moisture absorption
  • • Protect from direct heat and sunlight
  • • Veterinary feed supplement only
