Protective Plus

Product Overview

Protective Plus is a specialized granule-based formula designed to address deep-seated reproductive issues and complex infertility in dairy animals. It works to restore the uterine environment and support successful pregnancy.

Target Species

Cattle, Buffalo, and Camels.

Available Packing

6 Packets of Granules

Mode of Action

• Uterine Toning: Enhances the uterine lining to facilitate proper embryo implantation.

• Anti-Infective Action: Clears minor uterine infections that prevent successful conception.

Key Benefits

  • Resolves repeat breeding issues
  • Optimizes uterine environment
  • Supports embryo retention
  • Improves reproductive health

Dosage & Administration

Standard Treatment 1 Packet Daily

Administer one packet daily for 6 consecutive days. Mix with feed or a small amount of concentrate.

Storage & Safety

  • • Store in a cool, dry place
  • • Protect from moisture and direct sunlight
  • • For veterinary use only
