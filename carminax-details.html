Carminax Mixture

Product Overview

Fagas Carminax Mixture is a professional liquid formulation designed to treat various digestive upsets. It targets flatulence, indigestion, and bloating to restore health and productivity in livestock.

Target Species

Cattle, Buffalo, Sheep, Goats, and Calves.

Available Packing

450 ml Bottle

Key Benefits

  • Rapid relief from indigestion
  • Effective gas neutralization
  • Soothes stomach discomfort
  • Restores healthy gut motility

Dosage & Administration

Cattle & Buffalo 100 ml – 150 ml
Sheep & Goat 30 ml – 50 ml

* Administer orally. Repeat if necessary after 6 hours.

Storage & Safety

  • • Store below 30°C in a dry place
  • • Protect from direct sunlight
  • • For veterinary oral use only
