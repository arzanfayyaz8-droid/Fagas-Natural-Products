FAGAS SA-DCP

Product Overview

Fagas SA-DCP is a pharmaceutical-grade Di-Calcium Phosphate formulated to supply bioavailable calcium and phosphorus—two critical macro-minerals required for skeletal development, milk production, muscle contraction, enzymatic reactions, and reproductive efficiency.

Composition

Di-Calcium Phosphate (High Purity, Feed Grade)
Calcium & Phosphorus in optimal physiological ratio for livestock nutrition.

Key Benefits

  • Corrects calcium & phosphorus deficiencies
  • Strengthens bones, joints, and skeletal frame
  • Improves milk yield and milk solid content
  • Enhances feed conversion efficiency
  • Supports strong muscle contraction & nerve transmission
  • Prevents milk fever, rickets, and osteomalacia

Indications

  • • Weak bones or lameness
  • • Low milk production
  • • Delayed growth in calves
  • • Reproductive inefficiency due to mineral imbalance
  • • High-yielding dairy animals

Mode of Action

SA-DCP provides readily absorbable calcium and phosphorus that integrate into bone matrix formation, regulate enzymatic activity, and stabilize metabolic pathways essential for production, reproduction, and immunity.

Dosage & Administration

Dairy Cattle 50 – 100 g Daily
Beef Cattle 30 – 70 g Daily
Calves 20 – 30 g Daily

* Mix thoroughly with feed or mineral ration

Storage & Safety

  • • Store in a cool, dry place
  • • Protect from moisture
  • • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • • For veterinary use only
