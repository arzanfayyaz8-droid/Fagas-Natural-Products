Meat & Milk Grow

Product Overview

Fagas Meat & Milk Grow is a high-potency liquid performance enhancer designed for dairy and meat animals under commercial production systems. It improves nutrient absorption, accelerates weight gain, increases milk yield, and stabilizes metabolic activity during stress, growth, and peak lactation.

Formulation Highlights

  • Readily absorbable energy
  • Essential amino acids
  • Growth-supporting vitamins
  • Metabolic activators
  • Appetite stimulants

Key Benefits

  • Rapid and visible weight gain in beef animals
  • Significant increase in daily milk yield
  • Improved milk quality and fat percentage
  • Enhanced feed conversion ratio (FCR)
  • Reduced production stress and fatigue
  • Faster recovery after illness or calving
  • Improves appetite and digestion

Indications

  • • Low milk production
  • • Slow growth or poor weight gain
  • • Post-calving weakness
  • • Stress due to weather, transport, or disease
  • • Poor feed utilization
  • • Intensive fattening programs

Mode of Action

Meat & Milk Grow works by optimizing metabolic pathways responsible for energy production, protein synthesis, and nutrient assimilation. Its liquid form allows rapid absorption, leading to faster muscle deposition, improved lactation efficiency, and sustained productivity even under high physiological demand.

Dosage & Administration

Dairy Cattle & Buffalo 50 – 100 ml / Day
Beef Cattle / Fattening 80 – 120 ml / Day
Calves & Young Stock 20 – 40 ml / Day

* Can be mixed with feed or drinking water

Storage & Safety

  • • Store below 30°C
  • • Shake well before use
  • • Keep container tightly closed
  • • Veterinary nutritional supplement only
